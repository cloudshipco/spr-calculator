# Small Producer Relief Calculator

A simple calculator for UK Alcohol Duty rates with Small Producer Relief (SPR). Enter your product details and annual production volume to find your discounted duty rate.

Based on [GOV.UK guidance](https://www.gov.uk/guidance/how-to-work-out-your-alcohol-duty-rates-if-youre-eligible-for-small-producer-relief) — rates effective from 1 February 2026.

## Features

- Covers all product types: beer, cider (still & sparkling), wine, spirits, and other fermented products
- Draught and non-draught rates
- Step-by-step calculation breakdown showing the exact band, formula, and arithmetic
- Batch duty calculator to see duty payable on a specific volume
- Volume converter (product hectolitres → pure alcohol hectolitres)

## Usage

Open `index.html` in a browser — no build step or dependencies required.

## Disclaimer

This calculator is for guidance only. Always verify duty calculations with HMRC.
